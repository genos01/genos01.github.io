Little Known Graphic Design Tips

Little known graphic design tips

Very so often, get up, walk away from it and do something else for a while. You can be too close to your work to notice things if you're staring at it for hours on end. Apply a black and white adjustment layer as a way of quickly checking contrast. Photoshop Interface colours - always use the lightest setting of designing for mobile. This gives better context to design as mobile is often used in daylight. (After effects is dark grey because most watch movies in a dark environment.) CMD+S should be an involuntary, subconscious command very few minutes. if you're designing something to be built by someone else (UI, physical product, etc) you own the design of the product and not the mocks on your hard drive. No one cares how good the mocks are while using the product. Architects don't go around saying "hey, look at the plans I drew", they show off photos of the building.
